Project Pitch

Spark is an Android-first networking app that uses phone-to-phone NFC taps (with QR fallback) to share a consolidated profile, socials, resume, and website, instantly. Connections are logged and organized by event and time, and a map and analytics help you follow up intentionally.

Features

  • Update profile (photo, socials, website, resume planned)
  • Share via NFC or QR
  • Receive profiles easily
  • Manage connections with tags, map, and analytics
