Tap • Share • Grow

Share a single, polished profile with one tap. Spark logs connections, associates them to events, and helps you follow up so your network keeps growing.

Download APK View GitHub

Spark is a networking app that uses phone-to-phone NFC taps to share a consolidated profile, socials, resume, and website, instantly. Connections are logged and organized by event and time, and a map and analytics help you follow up intentionally.

Features

  • Update profile (photo, socials, website, resume planned)
  • Share via NFC or QR Code
  • Receive profiles easily
  • Manage connections with tags, map, and analytics
